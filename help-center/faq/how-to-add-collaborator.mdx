title: How do I add a collaborator?
description: Invite team members to work on an AuraWrite document.
---

## Overview

AuraWrite allows inviting others to collaborate on your docs.

## Steps

1. Open the document.
2. Click the "Share" button in the toolbar.
3. Enter your collaborator's email address.
4. Set their permission (View or Edit).
5. Click "Send Invite".

> The invitee will receive an email with a link to join your document.

<Callout kind="tip">
You can revoke access anytime via the same Share menu.
</Callout>
