---
title: Creating a New Document
description: Start a new project in AuraWrite.
---

## Steps
<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    ````
    Navigate to https://aura.guidoschmitter.com and log in.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'New Document'">
    ````
    Press the New Document button at the top right.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Title and Start Writing">
    ````
    Name your document and begin writing in the editor.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save Changes">
    ````
    Changes are saved automatically as you type.
    ````
  </Step>
</Steps>
