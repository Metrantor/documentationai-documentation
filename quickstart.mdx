## Prerequisites

Before starting, ensure you have:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge)
- An email address for account registration
- Stable internet connection

</Callout>

AuraWrite works entirely in the browser, so no downloads or installations are required.

## Create Your Account

Sign up in under a minute.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Site" icon="globe">
    Navigate to [https://app.aurawrite.com](https://app.aurawrite.com) and click **Sign Up**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details" icon="user">
    Provide your email, create a password, and complete the CAPTCHA.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox for a verification link and click it to activate your account.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log In" icon="log-in">
    Return to the site, enter your credentials, and access the dashboard.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Document

Build a simple project right away.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Document" icon="file-plus">
    Click **New Project** on the dashboard and select **Blank Document**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Type your title and start writing in the rich text editor. Use markdown shortcuts like `**bold**` or `# Heading`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Format Text" icon="type">
    Highlight text and use the toolbar for bold, italics, lists, or headings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
